Is the Answer on Screen?

Every game is 12 questions: easy → medium → hard, around and around, ending with the Special Question of the Day. The giant number up top is what the question is worth right now — and the table colour heats up with it:

LOW
MED
HIGH
SPECIAL

Each question hits the table with no answers showing. Press → (swipe left / "Hit me") to deal cards one at a time — up to 12. Lock in (Enter, button, or swipe down) when you think the newest card is the answer:

Correct — bank the worth, and every question after gets worth more
Near (1 card off) — 0 points, but the stakes hold
Wrong — 0 points, and the worth cools down (a blown easy hurts most)

Tap any card to preview its zones before you commit. Number questions deal a counting sequence — find your number and strike.
